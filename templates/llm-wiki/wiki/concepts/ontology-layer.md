# Ontology Layer

type: concept
concept_id: ontology_layer

## One-line Definition

The ontology layer defines the concept types, relation rules, and facts that make the wiki more consistent than free-form notes.

## Summary

The starter ontology separates T-Box and A-Box.

- T-Box: class and relation definitions
- A-Box: actual instances and facts
- Reasoner: consistency checks and simple inference
